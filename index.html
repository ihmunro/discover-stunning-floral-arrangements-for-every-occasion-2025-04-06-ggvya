Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master florist Isabella Chen, Bloom began as a small boutique in downtown Seattle with a vision to create extraordinary floral experiences. What started as a passion project has blossomed into the region's premier floral design studio, serving thousands of satisfied customers and earning numerous industry accolades for our innovative designs and commitment to excellence.

At Bloom, our mission is to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories through the artistry of flowers. We believe in sustainable practices, supporting local growers, and delivering unparalleled customer service. Our core values of creativity, quality, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do, from selecting the finest blooms to ensuring perfect delivery of every arrangement.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your delivery area and timing?

We deliver throughout the greater metropolitan area, with same-day delivery available for orders placed before 2 PM. Standard delivery hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Tuesday through Sunday. Specific delivery windows can be requested for an additional fee.

How long will my flowers last?

With proper care, our arrangements typically last 7-10 days. We provide detailed care instructions with every delivery and offer a 7-day freshness guarantee on all our arrangements.

Do you offer custom arrangements?

Yes! We specialize in custom designs for any occasion. Our expert florists will work with you to create the perfect arrangement matching your style, color preferences, and budget. Contact us for a consultation.

What is your cancellation policy?

Orders can be cancelled or modified up to 24 hours before the scheduled delivery time for a full refund. For same-day orders, cancellations must be made within 2 hours of placing the order.

Do you offer subscription services?

Yes, we offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly subscription plans for both residential and commercial clients. Subscribers enjoy priority service, exclusive designs, and preferential pricing.
